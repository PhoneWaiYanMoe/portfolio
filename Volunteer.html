Green Summer Volunteer 2024 - Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

In the summer of 2024, I had the incredible opportunity to volunteer for the Green Summer Campaign in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. As part of this initiative, I dedicated my time to teaching English to underprivileged children, helping them develop essential language skills. The experience was both rewarding and inspiring, as I witnessed the enthusiasm and progress of these young learners.

The program not only allowed me to contribute to the local community but also deepened my understanding of cultural exchange and education's impact. It was a meaningful journey filled with learning, teamwork, and unforgettable moments.
