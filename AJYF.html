Candidate of ASEAN-Japan Youth Festival 2023

I had the honor of being a candidate for the ASEAN-Japan Youth Festival 2023 held in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. This event brought together young leaders from across ASEAN and Japan to discuss pressing global issues, exchange cultures, and foster international collaboration.

As part of the program, I participated in panel discussions, workshops, and cultural exchanges that highlighted the importance of regional cooperation, sustainability, and innovation. It was an enriching experience that expanded my perspective on diplomacy, leadership, and cross-cultural communication.

The event was a stepping stone toward greater international networking and personal growth, leaving a lasting impact on my journey.
